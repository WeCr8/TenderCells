# Master Vision

Tender Cells is not a chicken coop company.

Tender Cells is an educational and agricultural innovation ecosystem.

Long-Term Goal:
Become the world's leading platform for teaching engineering through real-world animal care and agricultural automation projects.

Core Pillars:
1. Animal Care
2. Engineering Education
3. Agricultural Technology
4. Open Source Innovation
5. Workforce Development
6. Sustainability
7. Research
8. Community
