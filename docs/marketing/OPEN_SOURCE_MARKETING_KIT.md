# TenderCells Open Source Marketing Kit

Use this kit for the first open-source push. Keep the promise simple: build it yourself, improve it, repair it, simulate it, or buy a kit if you want the faster path.

## Short Announcement

TenderCells is now opening up as an open-source home farming automation platform. We are building software, firmware, hardware docs, product registration, and simulation tools for backyard animal care systems like Chicken TenderCell, Roaming Roost, WatchTower AI, Duck Dock, and custom community devices.

The goal is practical: people should be able to build, repair, customize, and simulate their own home animal tending systems without being locked into black-box hardware.

GitHub: https://github.com/WeCr8/TenderCells

## GitHub/Discord Post

We are building TenderCells as an open-source operating system for home farming automation and animal care.

What is included:

- React/Firebase dashboard and product registry.
- ESP32 firmware direction for coop, sensor, motor, and monitoring hardware.
- Product schemas for custom community builds.
- Property and product simulation planning.
- Documentation standards for BOMs, wiring, firmware, assembly, troubleshooting, API/MQTT, and safety validation.

Early product families:

- Chicken TenderCell automated coop.
- Roaming Roost mobile enclosure.
- WatchTower AI predator monitor.
- Duck Dock, Bunny Burrow, Pigeon Palace, Goat Guardian, and rail modules.
- Community custom products and 3D printed modules.

We are looking for builders, homesteaders, firmware folks, CAD/3D-print people, teachers, FFA/4H mentors, and robotics/simulation contributors.

Repo: https://github.com/WeCr8/TenderCells

## Reddit/Facebook Post

We are starting an open-source project called TenderCells for home farming automation and animal care.

The idea is to make backyard animal systems easier to build and repair: automated coop doors, feeders, water monitoring, predator cameras, property layout, mobile enclosures, and eventually simulation for custom hardware.

We are documenting the software, firmware, product registry, wiring/BOM needs, and build standards so people can create their own devices or contribute designs. We also plan to offer kits later for people who want the hardware bundled.

We would love feedback from chicken keepers, homesteaders, makers, teachers, and anyone building animal-care automation.

GitHub: https://github.com/WeCr8/TenderCells
